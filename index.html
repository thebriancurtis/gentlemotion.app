Gentle Motion TestFlight Now Open (Apple Watch, iPhone, iPad)

I am opening early access to Gentle Motion, a sensory motion app designed for people who find predictable, controlled motion helpful during moments of restlessness, especially those who are neurodivergent or who rely on fidget tools.

The app is intentionally minimal.
No menus to navigate. No choices to manage.
A single, predictable motion environment that responds smoothly and consistently to touch.

How to use it:

Swipe to move and tap to slow the motion.
On Apple Watch, turn the Digital Crown for a tactile experience.

I am looking for TestFlight testers who:

  • Use sensory tools or fidgets
  • Prefer low-demand, predictable interactions
  • Experience restlessness or sensory overload
  • Can share a few minutes of direct feedback

Gentle Motion runs on Apple Watch, iPhone, and iPad, with a watch-first design.
